My husky was named Loki.

I got Loki at the beginning of November of 2014. He was only seven weeks old and weighed about six pounds. From the beginning he was a handful to say the least. But after a lot of training and patience he because the best dog anyone could ask for. He loved running and playing fetch(when he was actually in the mood). He destroyed many a things, from shoes, tools or furniture. Unfortunately luck wasn't on our side and he developed lymphoma and it spread quickly. He passed away towards the end of 2023 after a short time after diagnosis. While I loved my dog very much, I had to let him go. The Siberian husky are an amazing breed, but need many people get them to only realize th challenge they bring.
